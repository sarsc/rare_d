There are more than 8000 rare diseases, affecting 350 million people globally. That equals the entire population of the United States.

It takes an average of 5 to 7 years, and over 7 different physicians, to find the right diagnosis.

In the process, patients can be misdiagnosed over 4 times ad go through about 15 different diagnostic tests.

Direct and indirect lifetime expenses of rare disease patients can go up to €1,000,000

Misdiagnoses in rare diseases are frequent because physicians often navigate the unknown.

We must create solutions to reduce the time needed to find the right diagnosis.

Our goal is to increase efficiency and reliability in diagnosing rare diseases.
